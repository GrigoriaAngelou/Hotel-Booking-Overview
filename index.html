Hotel Overview Dashboard

The dashboard below uses a dataset in kaggle.com which contains booking information for a city hotel and a resort hotel, and includes information such as when the booking was made, length of stay, the number of adults, children, and/or babies, and the number of available parking spaces, among other things.

Explaining the purpose of this dashboard

Making this dashboard I wanted to answers questions like:
1. How well each type of hotel performs, for instance are there many cancellations compared to the bookings that they have? Or from which country most bookings come from?
2. I would also, like to have a comparison between the two hotel types to see how different the customers behave in those.
3. And lastly, I would like to know the demographic differences of those hotel types. I made 5 different parameters (2 adults with kids, 1 adult with kids, 2 adults, solo traveler, no informations) based on the number of adults and children that every room had to see the behavior of easvh customer type on the reservation status.
